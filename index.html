The Ducati Panigale V4R

Coming in at just under 380 lbs , the 221 whp Panigale V4R propels its rider with godlike acceleration up to 199 mph, creatiing a feeling of flying with a cape.

Ducati has a long racing history, which many consider when searching for the perfect motorcycle.

Three words to describe the Panigale V4R:

  1. Exhilarating
  2. Revolutionary
  3. Safe
